Boba Places on the Ave!

As a boba connessieur, I drink a lot of boba and have visited many spots in the greater Seattle area! Here is some of my top choices if you're bringing a friend and they wanna check out the good bubble tea in the area.

Favorite Boba Place

TP Tea, 5/5

TP Tea still remains as my favorite boba place! Although it can be a bit pricier because of the credit card fee and how the toppings are a little more expensive, it's still my favorite. The tea quality is really smooth and you can taste the tea flavor. The QQ noodle and the golden jelly are my favorite toppings still, and it's great that they have coffee jelly! I also like how their default tea is a variation of oolong tea. My favorite drinks are the 3Q TP Milk Tea and the Fresh Fruit Tea with Chia Seeds and golden jelly. I like how I can get no sugar and no ice and still find the drink appetizing!
